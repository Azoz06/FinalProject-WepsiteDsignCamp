A unified digital platform for health practitioners specializing in the field of anesthesia registered with the Saudi Commission for Health Specialties in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It aims to support them to reach the highest level in the profession by providing services that enhance the efficiency and quality of their work and facilitating the procedures for practicing the profession.

Features

Personal display panel

Here you will find all your details, such as the names of your patients, all the reports you can view, and all the data of your medical team

Calendar of scientific events

Provide a dedicated electronic calendar that can display to every practitioner all information about the specialized conferences, courses, and so on

Sharing experiences

Sharing of scientific experiences through the channels of virtual communication between different groups of practitioners

Electronic services

Empowering the health practitioner with some electronic services for health

Job opportunities

View miscellaneous career opportunities by health practitioner specialty

Everything on mobile

Soon the TAKATOF application will be launched on Android or iOS devices, from which you can manage everything.
